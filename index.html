FAQ's

1.

How long will my unit take to make?

If I am providing the hair for your unit please allow up to 2.5 weeks.
If the hair is being coloured please allow up to 3 weeks.
If you are prividing your own hair please allow up to 1.5 weeks.
Often times the unit may be ready before there proposed dates, however these timespans are give or take
I will always let you know as soon as your unit is ready.

2.

How much is postage and packaging?

£10 - Standard. Cut off time is 4.30pm.
£15 - Next day. Cut off time is 3.30pm.

3.

Can I bring my own hair to you?

Yes, You Can! Just make sure you bring it no later than 7 days before you would need the unit ready as £15 fee will be added.

4.

Can I bring a +1 to my booking?

Unfortunately not, as space is limited it's best you come alone,
no friends/family or children please.

5.

How do I measure my head?

In inches ideally!
With a measuring tape place the beginning in front of your ear and wrap it flat around to find out the circumference.
To find out the "ear to ear" pleace the measuring tapein front of your ear laying it flat along your hairline until you reach the front of your other ear.
Use this picture to help with other measurements.

6.

Do you accept returns?

Due to the nature of wigs and for hygiene purpose I do not.
However if you have any issues at all feel free to reach out to me.

7.

What is your cancellation policy?

Once deposits are paid they are not refundable.
If you need to change the date/time of your appointment a new deposit will be required unless stated otherwise.
For custom units if you decide you no longer want the unit the deposit is not refundable.

8.

How long does an install last?

With proper care your install should last up to 2 weeks.
Tips on how to keep your install looking fresh will be given at your appointment.

9.

Will wearing wigs damage my hairline?

The short answer is no, as long as the wig is not too thight and it's installed & removed correctly no harm will come to your natural hair at all.
However if the unit is installed/removed incorrectly it can pull at your natural hair.
If in doubt just send me a message!

10.

How long does a closure wig last?

A closure wig can last for as long as you want it to as long as you are taking good care of the lace.
The bundles tend to stay unaffected and can last years, however it's fine the lace that tends to bald.
This means your lace can least anywgere between 3 months/1 year(sometimes longer).

11.

How long does a frontal wig last?

Similar to closure wigs, the bundles on yout frontal wig can last years and it's the lace you may need to watch out for.
Generally when frontals are worn and removed a few hairs are lost each time meaning they don't tend to last all that long.
I would say the average lifespan of a frontal is 6 months with mildly frequent use (it can be shorter or last longer).
